Shadow Monarc AKA
SUNG JIN WOO
(성진우)






Sung Jin-Woo (성진우) is the protagonist of Solo Leveling. He was previously known as the World’s Weakest Hunter and delved down the path of becoming the World’s Strongest Hunter after he was Reawakened and became a Player.

Jin-Woo was reawakened as a Player after surviving the Cartenon Temple and completing the secret quest “Courage of the Weak.” The mysterious System has allowed Jin-Woo to level up and increase his strength, unlike all hunters, who are unable to increase their strength unless reawakened.

S RANK
Blood-Red Commander
IGRIS
(이그리트)





Igris is a high-ranking commander in Sung Jinwoo's Shadow Army, known for his loyalty and exceptional combat skills. Originally a dungeon boss, Igris was defeated by Jinwoo and later recruited as a Shadow Soldier, becoming one of the strongest and most trusted members of his army. His rank in the Shadow Army is "Marshal," surpassed only by Baru and Bellion.

Igris once served the Shadow Monarch Ashborn during his war with the Rulers eons ago. After Ashborn decided to retire and find a human successor, he tasked Igris with testing his successor in battle when the time was right. After obtaining the Shadow Extraction skill, Jin-Woo successfully used it on BRCI(Blood Red Commander Igris) by appealing to his loyal personality. However, as he thought the knight's original name was too long, Jin-Woo decided to simply name the shadow Igris instead.

S RANK
Ant King AKA
BERU
(베르)






Beru is a pivotal character, initially an antagonist and later one of Sung Jin-woo's most powerful shadows. He is the reincarnation of the Ant King, a powerful leader of the insectoid creatures Jin-woo faced on Jeju Island. Beru possesses exceptional strength and abilities, including flight, healing, and a lethal poison attack.

After Jinwoo kills the Ant King, he extracts its shadow to add to his army. When the shadow displays the ability to talk to him and asks for a name, Jinwoo names him Beru, after the author of The Ants, and sends him out to go slaughter what was left of the ant colony.

La Sombra más Fuerte
BELLION
(벨리온)





Being the strongest shadow and the Grand Marshal of Sung Jinwoo's shadow army. He was born as a shadow of Ashborn, the previous Shadow Monarch, and was created from the fruit of the World Tree. Bellion is noted for his strength and combat skills, especially his mastery in the use of weapons such as swords and whips. He is recognized as the right-hand man of the Shadow Monarch and is capable of defeating powerful shadows such as Beru with ease.

Bellion is considered the ultimate shadow, and his power is comparable to that of the Monarchs. He is the Grand Marshal and holds the highest authority over the shadow army after Jin-Woo. He possesses superhuman strength and speed, advanced regeneration, and exceptional combat proficiency. He is adept at using weapons and can also fly.
